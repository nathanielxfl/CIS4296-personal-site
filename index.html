Slot Machine

Using React.Js (for the first time ever*), my partner and I created a slot machine web application as apart of a class project for client-side web-scripting. It was split into two sections: one for the player and another for the "employee". The player section housed the game, instructions, and leaderboard. The employee section housed the history of every game played behind a hard-coded login. Local storage is used to keep track of player's data as they play and logs it into the leaderboard and history for the employee to access.
